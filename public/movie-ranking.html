The Best Movies According to Noah

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

1. Dune Part 2

The ultimate sci-fi epic. It was surreal to watch after the anticipation built up by reading the book and watching Dune Part 1 two years prior.

2. The Batman

The atmosphere of this movie was amazing. I finally got a gritty mainstream superhero movie. The costume design was awesome too.

3. Paranormal Activity

This movie is really fun to watch with family. I was so scared the first time I watched it and the ending(s) does(do) not disappoint.
